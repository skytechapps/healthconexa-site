Privacy Policy for BuyOrNot AI

Skytech Apps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the BuyOrNot AI mobile application. This policy details how we handle your data to ensure transparency and protect your privacy.

1. CAMERA Permission

Our App requires access to your device's Camera to scan products and provide AI-driven analysis. Images captured during this process are used strictly for analysis and are NOT stored permanently on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save your scan history to your local device.

2. Accessibility Service (Shopping Assistant)

We utilize the Accessibility Service API to detect products and pricing while you are using shopping apps (such as Amazon, Flipkart, etc.). This allow us to provide real-time AI assistance. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any sensitive personal data, login credentials, or financial information via this service.

3. Usage Data

We may collect basic diagnostic data and anonymized usage statistics to help us improve app performance, stability, and the overall user experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
