Privacy Policy for SwarConnexa AI

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

SwarConnexa AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the SwarConnexa AI mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data via Google Health Connect

With your explicit permission, SwarConnexa AI reads health and fitness data from Google Health Connect, including:

  • Steps and activity data
  • Calories burned
  • Sleep data
  • Heart rate and other vitals

Manual Health Entries

You may manually enter health data such as:

  • Blood Pressure (BP)
  • Blood Sugar / Glucose
  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Weight and height

These entries are stored locally on your device and can be deleted by you at any time.

AI Insights Data

To generate insights, summarized and anonymized health trends (e.g., step averages, sleep duration patterns) may be processed temporarily.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Health dashboard: Display and organize your health data.
  • AI insights: Provide personalized health and fitness recommendations.
  • Local processing: Analyze trends directly on your device wherever possible.

3. Data Storage & Technical Architecture

  • On-device storage: Your health data (including manual entries like BP, Sugar, BMI) is stored locally on your device.
  • No central database: We do NOT store your personal or health data in any external database or server.
  • Stateless processing: AI requests may pass through secure cloud functions (e.g., Cloudflare Workers) which process data temporarily and do not store it.
  • Temporary data handling: Any data sent for AI insights is anonymized and processed in real-time only.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal health data.

  • Google Health Connect: Used for secure health data access.
  • AI Processing Services: Anonymized summaries may be processed for generating insights. No personal identity is shared.
  • Google AdMob: Used for ads. May collect device identifiers but does NOT access health data.

5. Data Security

  • Your data primarily stays on your device
  • No cloud database storage of personal health data
  • Secure communication used for any temporary processing

6. Your Control & Rights

  • Access your health data anytime within the app
  • Delete manual entries (BP, Sugar, BMI, etc.)
  • Disconnect Health Connect access anytime

7. Children's Privacy

SwarConnexa AI is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated date.
